## 2. What the project is (30 seconds)
The team has ~700 airway commensal strains and must down-select formulations that are SAFE, GROW, and exclude
*Pseudomonas aeruginosa* (PA). The evidence is scattered across many people's files. We built ONE strain-level
decision card via a bronze -> silver -> gold pipeline, organized around Adam's three gates: **Safety, Viability,
Competition**. Spencer + Alex own the data integration; the biologists own the thresholds and decisions.
